How to Enroll a Device
  1. Factory reset the Android device (or use a brand-new device).
  2. At the initial setup screen, tap the welcome screen 6 times to launch the QR code reader.
  3. Connect to Wi-Fi if prompted and ensure the device has internet access.
  4. Scan the QR code displayed on this page with the device.
  5. The device will automatically download the MDM client APK and configure itself as a managed device.
  6. Once provisioning completes, the device will appear on the Devices page.
